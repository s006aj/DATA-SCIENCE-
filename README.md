# DATA-SCIENCE-
Module end assignment 
ABC Company - Employee Data Analysis

## Project Overview
This project shows a comprehensive data analysis report of ABC Company, processing a dataset of 458 employees across 9 columns to uncover organizational trends, position segmentations, and salary distributions.

## Preprocessing Steps
* **Height Data Correction:** Modified the "Height" column by replacing with random integers between 150 and 180 to ensure data consistency and structural integrity for subsequent analytical tasks.

## Key Insights (Data Story)
* **Team Distribution:** The **[New orleans pelicans ]** team dominates the workforce, holding the highest percentage split, while **[Minnesota Timberwolfs]** represents the smallest division.
* **Position Segregation:** Employees are distributed across various roles, with **[SG]** emerging as the most common job title in the company.
* **Age Demographics:** The predominant age group among employees is **[25-29]**, showing that the company's workforce leans heavily toward young-to-mid career professionals.
* **Salary Expenditures:** * The highest total salary expenditure belongs to the **[Cleveland cavalirs]** team.
  * The highest total salary expenditure by role belongs to the **[C]** position.
* **Age vs. Salary Correlation:** The calculated Pearson correlation coefficient is **[0.21]**. This indicates a virtually non-existent relationship, proving that at ABC Company, compensation is determined by job role or performance rather than age.
